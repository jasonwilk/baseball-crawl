## Description
After this story is complete, the context layer includes a new `.claude/rules/perspective-provenance.md` rule that codifies the perspective provenance principle as a permanent invariant, and `data-model.md`, `architecture-subsystems.md`, and `CLAUDE.md` are updated to reference it. E-219 is marked ABANDONED.

## Context
This epic establishes a fundamental architectural principle: every piece of player data must carry perspective provenance. Without a context-layer rule, future loaders, parsers, or pipeline changes could silently regress by omitting `perspective_team_id`. The rule makes this a code-review checklist item.
