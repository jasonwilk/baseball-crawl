## Description
After this story is complete, the PM agent definition (`/.claude/agents/product-manager.md`) will describe PM's dispatch role as coordinator-via-messaging, not as a team/agent spawner. All references to PM using `TeamCreate`, `Agent` tool, or spawning teammates will be replaced with messaging-based coordination language. The read-only enforcement and implementation firewall sections remain intact.

## Context
The PM agent definition's "Dispatch Mode" and "Dispatch Procedure" sections currently describe PM creating teams and spawning implementing agents -- actions that are architecturally impossible for a teammate. The read-only enforcement added after E-051 is correct and stays. This story fixes the dispatch coordination model to match reality.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] **AC-1**: The "Dispatch Mode" section's "Your Role: Standing Team Coordinator" describes PM as a coordinator who communicates via `SendMessage`, not as an agent that spawns teammates. The phrase "When you create a dispatch team" is replaced.
- [ ] **AC-2**: The "Dispatch Procedure" steps no longer reference `TeamCreate` or `Agent` tool. Steps that previously said "spawn implementing agents" now say "assign stories to teammates via messaging" or equivalent. Steps that said "Create a team" are removed or replaced with "The team lead has created the team and spawned you along with implementing agents."
- [ ] **AC-3**: The procedure includes a new step for cascading: "If newly unblocked stories require an agent type not on the team, message the team lead to spawn the additional agent."
- [ ] **AC-4**: The "Team lifecycle" bullet under "You own during dispatch" is updated from "spawning implementers" to "assigning stories to implementers, sending them back if criteria are unmet, requesting additional spawns from team lead when needed."
- [ ] **AC-5**: The "Implementation Firewall" and "Anti-Patterns" sections are preserved. Anti-pattern #5 ("Never self-implement") remains. No new anti-patterns are needed since the spawn prohibition is now structural (PM has no Agent tool).
- [ ] **AC-6**: The "Context Block Format" section is preserved -- it describes what goes into the context, not who spawns whom.
- [ ] **AC-7**: The tools list in the frontmatter remains `Read`, `Glob`, `Grep` only (no `Agent` or `TeamCreate`). This is already correct -- verify it is not changed.
- [ ] **AC-8**: No remaining references to PM "spawning" agents, "creating teams", or using `TeamCreate`/`Agent` tool anywhere in the file. (Exception: "request team lead to spawn" is acceptable.)
