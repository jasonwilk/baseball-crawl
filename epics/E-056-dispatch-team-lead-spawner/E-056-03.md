# E-056-03: Update Implement Skill for Team Lead Spawning

## Epic
[E-056: Fix Dispatch Pattern -- Team Lead as Spawner](epic.md)

## Status
`TODO`

## Description
After this story is complete, the implement skill (`/.claude/skills/implement/SKILL.md`) will have the team lead spawn PM AND all implementing agents listed in the epic's Dispatch Team section -- not just PM alone. The team lead's role expands from "spawn PM and step back" to "spawn the full team and remain available for additional spawn requests."

## Context
The implement skill currently has the team lead spawning only PM, then "stepping back" while PM supposedly spawns implementers. Since PM cannot spawn teammates, this leaves the team with only PM and no implementers. The fix: the team lead reads the Dispatch Team section (it already does this in Phase 1) and spawns all listed agents alongside PM.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] **AC-1**: Phase 1 (Team Composition) is preserved -- the team lead already reads the Dispatch Team section. No changes needed here.
- [ ] **AC-2**: Phase 2 (Dispatch) is rewritten. The team lead now: (a) creates the team via `TeamCreate`, (b) spawns PM with the epic context, (c) spawns each agent type listed in the Dispatch Team section (or from the routing table if no Dispatch Team section exists). Each implementer receives a brief initial context: "You are on the dispatch team for epic E-NNN. The PM will assign your stories via messaging. Wait for the PM's instructions."
- [ ] **AC-3**: The "step back" instruction is replaced. After spawning all agents, the team lead "remains available for spawn requests from PM" but does not actively coordinate. If PM messages the team lead requesting an additional agent, the team lead spawns it.
- [ ] **AC-4**: The context block for PM spawn includes a roster of who else is on the team: "Teammates spawned: [agent-type-1], [agent-type-2], ..." so PM knows what agents are available.
- [ ] **AC-5**: The Workflow Summary diagram is updated to show: team lead spawns PM + implementers, PM coordinates via messaging, team lead available for additional spawns.
- [ ] **AC-6**: Anti-pattern #1 ("Do not bypass PM") is preserved but the explanation is updated: the team lead spawns agents but does NOT assign stories, verify ACs, or make routing decisions. Those remain PM's job.
- [ ] **AC-7**: The edge case for "PM Spawn Fails" is preserved. A new edge case is added: "Implementer Spawn Fails" -- if spawning an implementer fails, the team lead reports the failure to PM (via SendMessage) and to the user.
- [ ] **AC-8**: When the Dispatch Team section is absent or empty, the team lead spawns PM only and tells PM: "No Dispatch Team section found. You will need to determine required agents and message me to spawn them." This handles the fallback case gracefully.

## Technical Approach

Add the spawn-failure edge case. Use the rewritten `/.claude/rules/dispatch-pattern.md` (E-056-01) as the authoritative reference.
