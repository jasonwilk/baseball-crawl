## Description
After this story is complete, `/.claude/rules/dispatch-pattern.md` will accurately describe the Agent Teams architecture: the team lead creates the team and spawns all agents (PM + implementers); PM coordinates via `SendMessage` but never spawns agents or creates teams. The false claim that "teammates can spawn other teammates" will be removed, and the dispatch flow will show the team lead as the spawner throughout.

## Context
This is the foundational story -- it rewrites the authoritative dispatch rules document. Stories 02-05 depend on this story for the canonical pattern description. The current file contains multiple incorrect statements about PM spawning agents and creating teams, plus a false architectural claim about teammate spawning capabilities.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] **AC-1**: The "How Dispatch Works" section correctly states that only the team lead can create teams and spawn teammates. The false claim "Teams let any agent spawn teammates, who can in turn spawn other teammates -- no nesting limits" is removed and replaced with the correct constraint.
- [ ] **AC-2**: The "Team Lead Dispatch Boundary" section is rewritten. The team lead is no longer prohibited from spawning implementing agents -- it is now REQUIRED to. The team lead spawns PM + all implementers listed in the epic's Dispatch Team section. The prohibitions on status updates, AC verification, and routing decisions remain (those are still PM's job).
- [ ] **AC-3**: The "Team Composition" section describes two spawning scenarios: (a) team lead spawns PM + all implementers at team creation based on the Dispatch Team section, (b) team lead spawns additional agents mid-dispatch when PM requests them for newly unblocked stories.
- [ ] **AC-4**: The "PM Responsibilities During Dispatch" section replaces all "spawn" language with "coordinate via messaging" / "assign stories to teammates" / "request team lead to spawn additional agents." PM never uses `TeamCreate` or `Agent` tool.
- [ ] **AC-5**: The "Dispatch Flow" numbered steps are rewritten: team lead creates team and spawns all agents (steps 1-4), PM coordinates from there via messaging (steps 5+). The flow shows PM requesting spawns from team lead when cascading.
- [ ] **AC-6**: The "PM Is Read-Only" section is preserved but updated to remove any implication that PM spawns agents. The status-update delegation pattern remains correct.
- [ ] **AC-7**: The "Closure Sequence" steps are preserved in substance but updated so PM instructs teammates (via messaging) rather than "spawning" anything.
- [ ] **AC-8**: The "Task Tool vs. Agent Teams" section is updated. The Task tool remains available to PM for consultations. Agent Teams dispatch goes through the team lead as spawner.
- [ ] **AC-9**: The Context Packaging section is preserved unchanged (it describes what goes INTO the context block, not who spawns whom).
- [ ] **AC-10**: The file has no remaining references to PM using `TeamCreate`, `Agent` tool, or "spawning" agents. (Exception: PM may "request" that team lead spawn an agent.)
