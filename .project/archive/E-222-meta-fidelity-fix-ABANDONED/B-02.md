## Description
After this story is complete, `code-reviewer.md` will have a new Invariant Audit Mode section, two new Bug Pattern Checklist items (Invariant Spot-Check and Schema Fixture Parity), and an updated Review Procedure Step 2 that loads invariant declarations from the review assignment. Per-story CR could not see the cross-cutting surface area.
