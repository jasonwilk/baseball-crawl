## Summary
One or two sentences. What is the idea? Write it so you could read this in 6 months and immediately know what you were thinking.

## Why It Matters
What problem does this solve? Who benefits? Why would we want this eventually?

## Rough Timing
When might we need this? What would trigger us to promote this to an epic?

Examples:
- "Before dashboard goes live to coaching staff"
- "After E-003 is complete -- we'll feel the pain then"
- "Someday / nice to have -- no urgency"

## Dependencies & Blockers
What must be true before this idea is viable?

- [ ] Example: E-003 (data model) must be complete
- [ ] Example: Must have at least one full season of data loaded

## Open Questions
Things we'd need to figure out before this could become a real epic.

- Question 1
- Question 2

## Notes
Any additional context, links, prior art, or related ideas.
