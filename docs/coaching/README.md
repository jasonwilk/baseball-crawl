# LSB Baseball Analytics -- Coaching Guide

Welcome to the Lincoln Standing Bear High School baseball analytics system. This guide is for coaches and staff who want to understand what the system does and how to use it.

## What This System Does

The LSB baseball analytics system collects game data from GameChanger and organizes it so you can make better decisions before and during games. Think of it as having someone go through every box score from every game -- yours and your opponents' -- and put together the numbers you actually need.

Here is what it gives you:

- **Batting stats** for your players and opponents: on-base percentage, slugging, strikeout rate, walk rate, and more
- **Pitching stats**: strikeouts per nine innings, walks per nine, and other measures of effectiveness
- **Splits**: How players perform at home versus away, and against left-handed versus right-handed pitchers
- **Opponent scouting**: Lineup tendencies, key players to watch, and patterns you can exploit
- **Spray charts**: Ball-in-play direction for every batter -- useful for defensive positioning
- **Player development tracking**: How your players are improving (or struggling) across seasons and levels -- freshman, JV, varsity, and beyond

The system does not invent new data. It takes the same information you could get by opening every GameChanger box score by hand, and it organizes it all in one place so you can find what you need quickly.

## How to Access the Dashboard

The coaching dashboard is live at `https://bbstats.ai`. Jason will share your login with you directly.

From the dashboard you can:

- See your team's full schedule and check which upcoming opponents have scouting data ready
- Open an opponent's page to review their pitching staff and lineup tendencies before a game
- Print a scouting report or save it as a PDF to take into the dugout
- View spray charts showing where each batter tends to hit the ball

## Stats Update Automatically

When Jason adds a new team or resolves a duplicate, the system starts updating stats in the background right away -- no extra steps needed. When you open an opponent page, the coverage line near the top tells you exactly what data is behind the numbers: "Through [date] ([N] games)". That tells you both how current the data is and how many games it is based on.

## Who Maintains It

Jason manages the system behind the scenes. If something looks wrong with the data, if an opponent's page shows no data, or if you have any questions, talk to Jason.
