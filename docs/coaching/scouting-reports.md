# Scouting Reports

## Coming Soon

The scouting report feature is currently in development. When the coaching dashboard is ready, you will be able to pull up a scouting report on any upcoming opponent directly from the web.

This page describes what those reports will contain so you know what to expect.

---

## What a Scouting Report Will Include

### Opponent Lineup Tendencies

- Their typical batting order and how often it changes
- Which players bat in which spots across recent games
- Whether they platoon players (swap hitters based on your pitcher's handedness)
- Lineup changes between home and away games

### Key Players to Watch

- Their top hitters by on-base percentage and slugging
- Their ace pitcher(s) and closer, if they have one
- Who is hot and who is cold based on recent games
- Notable base stealers or bunters

### Pitching Matchup Information

- Their probable starter: strikeout rate, walk rate, and overall effectiveness
- How their starter performs against left-handed versus right-handed batters
- Their bullpen options and typical usage patterns
- Pitch count tendencies -- when do they pull their starter?

### Historical Matchups

- Results from prior games between LSB and this opponent (if available)
- How your hitters have performed against their pitchers in previous meetings
- Trends across the season -- is this team improving, declining, or steady?

### Suggested Adjustments

- Lineup recommendations based on the matchup
- Players who have favorable or unfavorable matchup histories
- Situational notes: when to run, when to play it safe, what to watch for

## How to Use Scouting Reports

Once the dashboard is live, Jason will share access with the coaching staff. Reports will be available before each game so you can review them during pre-game preparation.

If you need scouting information before the dashboard is ready, ask Jason -- he can pull the relevant numbers for you.
